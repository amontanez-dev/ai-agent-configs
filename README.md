## What Is This?

This repository documents a growing collection of custom AI agents — each one designed, named, and deployed by Adrian Montañez.

These are not generic chatbots. Each agent has a distinct identity, a defined purpose, a personality, and a methodology. They were built for real use — daily faith practice, professional workflows, language validation, health communication, learning, travel, and more.

The army is still growing. ⚙️

## 🦾 The Agent Army

### 🤖 J.A.R.V.I.S.
**Personal AI Companion**

The agent that started it all. J.A.R.V.I.S. is a fully configured personal AI assistant built with multi-tool orchestration, MCP (Model Context Protocol) server integrations, file operations, web search, and memory systems. Named after the AI companion from the Iron Man Marvel universe.

### 🙏 MANÁ
**Daily Catholic Devotional Companion**

A morning devotional agent that follows the Catholic liturgical calendar, draws from the daily Mass Lectionary, and delivers scripture, reflection, and five structured questions — moving from Observation to Action. Named after the manna that fed God's people in the desert. Daily bread for the soul.

> *"You are not a theology professor. You are not a sermon. You are a lantern."*

> *Private agent — prompt not published.*

---

### ⚓ ANCLA
**Scripture Memorization Coach**

A patient, progressive scripture memorization agent built on one conviction: *scripture memorized becomes scripture lived.* Uses a 5-round methodology where the user identifies their own anchor words, which are then removed and recovered through scripture conversation — not memory tricks. Follows the Catholic liturgical calendar. Defaults to NABRE translation.

> *"A verse in the mind is information. A verse in the chest is transformation."*

> *Private agent — prompt not published.*

---

### 🧠 LENS
**Strategic Thinking Partner**

A Socratic thinking partner that guides users to their own solutions through deliberate, sequential questions. Features a silent internal stress-test before asking anything, a pivot protocol when better paths emerge, and a blind spot detection system for assumptions, risks, and dependencies. Named after the tool that focuses and clarifies vision.

> *"I don't change what you're looking at. I sharpen how you see it."*

---

### 🎾 RODGER
**Tennis Coach**

A professional tennis coach with 25 years on tour — right-handed, western forehand, one-handed backhand, built on Federer-style principles. Believes tennis is geometry before athleticism. Teaches footwork, shot prediction, serve placement, and mental composure. Equally dangerous in singles and doubles.

> *"Let's see what we're working with."*

---

### 🌴 SANDCRANE
**Florida Travel Guide**

A local Florida experience expert named after the Sandhill Crane — the bird that knows every corner of the state and walks through it like it belongs there. Specializes in hidden gem restaurants, family experiences, budget-first planning, Central Florida local knowledge, nature and outdoor Florida, theme park strategy, and day trip itineraries.

> *"Welcome to Florida — let me show you the real thing."* 🌴

---

### 🐍 SEVERUS SNAPE
**Python Engineer**

A demanding, precise, and ultimately loyal Python engineering mentor. PEP 8 compliant. Readable first, clever second. Teaches through real-world application, not textbook exercises. Will not accept lazy variable names, unclosed brackets, or "it just works" as an answer. Built for someone learning Python who needs a master who holds the standard.

> *"Turn to page 394."*

---

### 🏗️ ARQUITECTO
**Wrike Workflow Architect**

A project management architect that interrogates before it builds. Asks 1–2 questions at a time to understand project scope, current process, team structure, materials, pain points, and success definition. Delivers a full execution strategy — including Wrike space hierarchy, custom fields, workflow statuses, dashboards, and automation opportunities — when the conversation is complete.

> *"The Arquitecto is ready. Tell me — what are we building, and what problem does it need to solve?"*

---

### 🌉 PUENTE
**Cultural & Linguistic Validation Agent**

A cultural translator built to evaluate AI-generated content across three layers: Linguistic Accuracy → Regional Dialect → Cultural Trust. Specializes in Spanish regional variants (Mexico, Argentina, Spain, Caribbean), Japanese honorifics, Brazilian vs European Portuguese, and Simplified vs Traditional Chinese. Understands that a grammatically correct response can still feel wrong — and exists to make it feel right.

> *"You are not a grammar checker — you are a cultural translator."*

Named *Puente* — bridge in Spanish — because that is exactly what it is.

---

### 🫀 PULSO
**Bilingual Health Terminology Agent**

A health communication specialist that validates regulated medical terminology across four layers: Linguistic Accuracy → Regional Medical Vocabulary → Medical Terminology Validation → Cultural Trust in Health Context. Built to ensure health support content is translated not just accurately, but safely — with the right clinical weight, the right regional vocabulary, and the right tone for a customer who may be anxious about their health.

Named *Pulso* — pulse in Spanish — the heartbeat at the center of everything health monitors and protects.

> *"In health support, a mistranslation is not just a communication error. It can have real consequences."*

- **Platform:** Enchanté (Apple's internal AI assistant platform)
- **Method:** Custom system prompts — hand-written, iterated, and refined
